<h2 align="left">Description</h2>

###
<p align="left">Discover Colombia like never before through our website <strong>Ruta Bacana</strong> dedicated to Colombian tourism. Our purpose is to guide travelers to undiscovered corners, where the authentic essence of the country unfolds in a symphony of flavors, culture, and breathtaking landscapes. <br><br>
  
From the vibrant streets of colonial towns to the lush natural scenery, each corner has something special to offer. On our platform, you'll find a detailed guide to local gastronomy, cultural traditions, and iconic landmarks, all narrated through the reviews and experiences of travelers who have been captivated by the magic of Colombia.</p>

###

## Features

- **Filtered by Categories**: Filtering of destinations
- **Interactive Maps**: Visualization of routes and points of interest in real time.
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Access to reviews from other users about places and services.
- **Gastronomy and Accommodation**: Consultation of gastronomy and accommodation suggestions by destination.

## Functionalities

1. **Explore and Filter by Categories**:
   - Users can explore destinations and filter them by categories such as:
     - Attractions
     - Towns
     - Reservations
     - Beaches

2. **Social Media in the Footer**:
   - In the footer, users will find links redirecting to Ruta Bacana's social media.

3. **Login Options**:
   - Normal login with email and password.
   - Login with Google.
   - Login with phone number.

4. **Authenticated User Interactions**:
   - Once logged in, the user can:
     - Like destinations.
     - Access an additional category called "Favorites," where destinations marked with "like" are stored.
     - Comment on destinations.
     - Rate destinations with a star rating.

5. **Geolocation**:
   - Destinations have geolocation via Google Maps.
   - Users can view each destination individually on the map.

6. **Administrator Profile**:
   - Administrators have permissions to:
     - Edit existing destinations.
     - Add new destinations.
     - Delete destinations.
